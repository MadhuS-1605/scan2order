## 1. Executive summary

Scan to Order is an all-in-one platform that lets your guests **scan a QR code,
browse your menu, order and pay from their own phone** — while your kitchen,
floor, billing, and reporting run on one connected system. No app for guests to
install, no new hardware to buy.

It works for a single café and for a multi-property hotel group, and it's built
India-first: **GST, GSTIN verification, FSSAI, UPI, WhatsApp bills, and English /
Hindi / Kannada** out of the box.

**Outcome for you:** faster table turns, fewer order errors, quicker payments,
higher repeat visits, and real-time visibility into your numbers — with less
work for your staff.

## 3. Solution overview

A guest's journey is **scan → order → pay → review**, and everything behind it is
automated. See **[flows.md](./flows.md)** for the full visual journeys.

**Guest:** scans the table QR → live menu on their phone → orders → tracks status
→ pays online / UPI / counter → gets a GST bill on WhatsApp or email → leaves a
rating.

**Your team:** orders arrive on the **Kitchen Display** (and **Bar Display**)
instantly (or after a waiter confirms), the **floor view** shows table status,
the **bill is shared per table**, and the day closes with a **Z-report** and an
optional **summary email**.

## 5. Why it fits your venue

### Restaurants
Table-shared billing, KDS, floor view, reservations and waiter-confirm keep
full-service dining smooth. Specials and modifiers drive upsell; analytics show
your top items and busiest hours.

### Cafés / QSR / food courts
Self-service, pay-first ordering with pick-up by number — guests order and pay
before the counter, cutting queues. Happy-hour pricing and loyalty bring them
back.

### Bars
A dedicated bar display routes drinks separately from food, with KOT printing and
per-item "ready" tracking.

### Hotels
Room charges post to a folio and settle at checkout; banquet bookings convert to
orders; manage every outlet (restaurant, café, banquet) from one multi-property
login with its own menu and branding.

### Cloud kitchens / delivery brands
Takeaway and delivery with address capture and fulfilment choice.

## 8. Security & compliance

- **Payments** handled by Razorpay; the platform verifies every payment (signature
  + server-side webhook) so nothing is left in limbo.
- **GST** compliant tax invoices with CGST/SGST split; **GSTIN** verified against
  the GST network; **FSSAI** printed on bills.
- **Roles & permissions** restrict staff access; an **audit log** records sensitive
  changes.
- **Geofencing** options prevent off-site/prank orders.
- Each venue gets an isolated subdomain and its own data.
