### QR ordering, payments & operations for restaurants, cafés & hotels

## The problem

Paper menus go stale. Waiters run back and forth to take orders.
Bills are slow. Payments are manual. Owners fly blind on numbers.

**Guests wait. Staff burn out. Revenue leaks.**

---

## The solution

Guests **scan a QR**, order and pay from their **own phone**.
Orders hit the **kitchen instantly**. Bills are **automatic & GST-ready**.
You get **live numbers** and **less work** — no app, no new hardware.

```
   📱 Scan  →  🍽️ Order  →  👨‍🍳 Kitchen  →  💳 Pay  →  ⭐ Review
```

## How it works — in 30 seconds

1. **Scan** the table QR — menu opens instantly (no app install)
2. **Order** — add to cart, send to kitchen (auto or waiter-confirmed)
3. **Track** — live status on the guest's phone
4. **Pay** — online, UPI scan-to-pay, or at the counter
5. **Bill** — itemised GST bill via WhatsApp / email / PDF
